What's a funnel, exactly?

A funnel is a simple automated path: a person sees your ad → they land on a page and sign up for your free resource → they get an email delivering that resource → they receive a short series of emails getting to know you and your services. By the time they're ready to book, they already trust you. You didn't have to be "on" for any of it.

You bring your expertise. We handle the strategy, the copy, the design, and the tech. And we don't just hand you something and disappear — you get a video walkthrough of everything we built, plus 30 days of Slack support for questions, tweaks, and troubleshooting.

This is the free resource that gives someone a reason to join your email list — a PDF guide, checklist, meal plan, or worksheet that solves a specific problem for your ideal client. Opt-In Page

A simple, clean page where people sign up to receive your lead magnet. We write the copy, design the page, and connect it to your email platform so signups flow in automatically. It doesn't need to be fancy — it needs to be clear and it needs to convert.

Step 3

Thank You Page

The page people land on immediately after signing up. It confirms they're in, tells them what to expect, and gives you an opportunity to invite them to take the next step — like booking a discovery call. A small thing that makes a real impression.

Delivery Email

The automated email that sends your lead magnet the moment someone signs up. It goes out immediately, at any hour, whether you're with a client or asleep. First impressions matter, and this one is handled.

Step 5

Welcome Email Series (5 emails)

A short sequence of emails that introduces who you are, what you do, and why it matters — building trust with new subscribers over the first week or two. These emails do the relationship-building work so that when someone is ready to book, they already know and trust you.
